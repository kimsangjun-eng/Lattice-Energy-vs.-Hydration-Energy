Solubility Tug-of-War

Lattice energy vs. hydration energy — which one wins decides if it dissolves

L-8
Solubility of an ionic compound is a tug-of-war between two attractions: ① how strongly the ions pull on each other (lattice energy), and ② how strongly water pulls on each ion (hydration energy). Whichever side is stronger decides the outcome — bigger lattice energy means insoluble, bigger hydration energy means soluble.
